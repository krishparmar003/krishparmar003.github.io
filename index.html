/01Research · Healthcare AI

Aphasia Detection & Severity Prediction

End-to-end ML pipeline that detects aphasia and classifies its severity from clinical speech transcripts. Built on acoustic and linguistic features (MFCCs, pitch, jitter, formants, speech rate) from the AphasiaBank corpus. Three architectures trained and compared — CNN, GRU, and a hybrid CNN+GRU. Developed during a research internship at IIT.

/06Scientific ML · Drug/Dye Design

NIR Fluorophore Designer

An ML tool that predicts the optical properties of near-infrared fluorophores — dyes that emit above 600 nm, the most useful region for deep-tissue bioimaging — and generates novel candidate molecules. Paste a SMILES string and a solvent to predict absorption, emission, quantum yield, and molar absorptivity; or set an objective and let it design and rank brand-new NIR dyes. An educational reimplementation inspired by the FLAME framework (Nature Communications, 2025), focused on the long-wavelength subset, with a gradient-boosting vs. GNN model comparison.

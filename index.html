Research · Healthcare AI

Aphasia Detection & Severity Prediction

End-to-end ML pipeline that detects aphasia and classifies its severity from clinical speech transcripts. Built on acoustic and linguistic features (MFCCs, pitch, jitter, formants, speech rate) from the AphasiaBank corpus. Three architectures trained and compared — CNN, GRU, and a hybrid CNN+GRU. Developed during a research internship at IIT.
